Ariadne Capture Party – Roles & Responsibilities

Current & Future Role Split

Role	Who	Responsibility	Level of Autonomy
Owner / Strategist	Benjamin	High-level direction, final decisions on big changes, ratify high-impact actions	Decision layer
Operational Coordinator	Guild Master	Turns goals into quests, assigns work to heroes, reports progress, manages pWin	High autonomy (with oversight)
Specialist Operators	Healer, Artificer, Mage, Knight, Rogue, Bard	Execute the actual work inside Ariadne-Capform (code, research, risk, writing, visuals, etc.)	Execute within defined boundaries

How It Should Work Long-Term

Benjamin gives direction and makes key decisions.
The Guild Master breaks intent into quests and manages the party.
The heroes execute the work (fixing, improving, building features, researching, etc.).
Benjamin is only pulled in for ratification on high-risk or high-impact changes (as defined in the rules).
This structure represents the intended end state of the "Ariadne Capture Party" system.
